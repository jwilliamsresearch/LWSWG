    • Demonstrator of Integrated Place Representations within Leisure Walking Systems

    Planned completion for Month Two

    The second month (November) will support the demonstration of a custom system integrating multiple datasets.

    A Basic Demonstrator of Integrated Data from Multiple Organisations

    Through engagement with interested parties a basic demonstrator system will be designed to enable integrated representations of place.

    This demonstrator will work with interested organisations to share the opportunities relating to integrated representations of place in leisure walking systems.

    This impact activity has not yet been completed. Please return to this page at the end of November for an update.

    This activity was supported by the Horizon CDT Impact Grant, made possible through funding from the Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council (EPSRC) [grant number EP/S023305/1].
