    Planned completion for Month One

    By the end of the first month of the project (October) a website and GitHub for the digital presence of the work will be completed.

    Developing a Website for Promoting the Impact of the LWSWG

    Part of the impact activity will promote the work completed as part of the PhD and organisation of the LWSWG through a digital presence, this includes the website and webpages presented on lwswg.jwilliams.science.

    The website will provide a long-term base for the knowledge found during the project, which will be updated when needed.

    Impact Progress

    The LWSWG website is currently in development and is accessible at present. The website was developed using Bootstrap and designed to share the role of the LWSWG and the wider impact of previous work. The website (pictured right) plays an important role in the long-term planning of the working group, and the archival of the results of the impact grant.

    This impact activity has not yet been fully completed, please browse the website to find the current progress.

    Key Impact Outputs

    Development of a Website

    The website supports the long-term sharing of the LWSWG overall project. The website is organised and hosted on GitHub Pages. The website will be updated throughout the project.
