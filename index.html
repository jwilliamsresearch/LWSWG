    This website is currently a work-in-progress, all content and information is subject to change.

    The Leisure Walking Systems Working Group (LWSWG) is an effort to understand the challenges in shared an interoperable representations of place in leisure walking systems. The working group is working towards a group of leisure walking researchers and practitioners interested in better representing place in leisure walking systems.

    The group seeks to draw members from private, public, and third sector organisations to form a new understanding of leisure walking applications and applicable challenges.

    Supporting Documentation

    The discussions and content of the LWSWG is open and available to view on GitHub.
